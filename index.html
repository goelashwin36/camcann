INTRODUCTION

We, at Camcann, aim to eliminate the limitations of current facial recognition technology like 3D spoofing, accuracy and response time issues. We propose a combination of hardware and software which captures and plots 3D mesh of faces with angle-wise photos in a universal format as well as enabling real-time facial recognition, emotion recognition, partially covered facial recognition and much more.
